reading the charts



the main scatter plot

If you've arrived here without a ticker in mind, the scatter plot is the key to all of the tickers included in the table. The plot has four regions with Trailing R-Squared images for each selected ticker shown below. Briefly, tickers appearing near the origin have not demonstrated a meaningful divergence from the baseline r-squared based on sentiment scoring - #4 TSLA is a good example of this condition. Tickers falling along the line where All sentiment is similar to Cluster sentiment reflect an improvement to r-squared for both All comments registering a sentiment score and those surfaced by a Clustering process - #1 CCL. Tickers appearing to the right of the line show promise on the All sentiment, but that the surfacing technique is not yielding any added value needed to product the additional r-squared - #2 PTON. Lastly, those to the left of the line show promise from the Clustered process but the All sentiment is less helpful - #3 AMC.

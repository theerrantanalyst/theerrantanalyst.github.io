FinChat Sentiments

Welcome! FCS attempts to use social financial-chat sentiment scores to explain divergent log-return behaviour of single stocks to those of a benchmark ETF. Source data is downloaded from the Reddit API, primarily from wallstreetbets. Other finance-related subreddits are included to broaden the range of ideas in the analysis. A guide to using the charts can be found here. Additional information can be found here.

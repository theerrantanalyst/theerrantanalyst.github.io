FinChat by Numbers

Each trading day thousands of comments are posted by traders of all kinds. They opine on general market conditions. They share specific stock trade ideas. They post screen-shot documentation of successes and failures. Each twenty-four hour cycle yields a novel-length collection of threads, non-sequiturs and mixed-media posts. Some is pure nonsense. And some may or may not give a glimpse into meaningful clusters of informally organised trade activity. FCbyN offers a quantitive view of the comments in three ways: 1- comment frequency by ticker, 2- mean sentiment score by ticker, and, 3- a natural language processing (nlp) view of sentiment surfaced through clustering and dimension reduction.

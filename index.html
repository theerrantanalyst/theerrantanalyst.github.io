FinChat Sentiments

Welcome to FCS. This project attempts to use social financial-chat sentiment scores to explain divergence of single stock returns to those of a benchmark ETF. Source data is downloaded from the Reddit API, primarily from wallstreetbets. Other finance-related subreddits are included to broaden the range of ideas in the analysis.

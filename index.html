FinChat by Numbers

Each trading day thousands of comments are posted by traders at all experience and capital levels. Each twenty-four hour cycle yields a novel-length collection of threads, non-sequiturs and mixed-media posts. Some material appears more toward an airing of grievances. And, some may or may not give a glimpse into meaningful clusters of informally-organised trade activity.
FCbyN offers a quantitive view of finanical chat downloaded from the Reddit API. Much of the commentary is sourced from the wallstreetbets subreddit. Given the bullish sentiment enherent in wsb, other subreddits have been included in an attempt to broaden the coverage of ideas shared. Details of source data can be found here.
A quantitiative view is presented in three ways:
1- comment frequency by ticker
2- mean sentiment score by ticker for all comments flagged as having the ticker detected
3- a natural language processing (nlp) view of sentiment, surfaced through a clustering and dimension reduction process

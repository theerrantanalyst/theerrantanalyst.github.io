FinChat by Numbers

Each trading day thousands of comments are posted by traders of all kinds. They opine on general market conditions. They share specific stock trade recommendations. Each twenty-four hour cycle yields a novel-length collection of threads, non-sequiturs and mixed-media posts. Some is pure nonsense. And some may or may not give a glimpse into meaningful clusters of informally organised trade activity.

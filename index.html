FinChat by Numbers

Source data for FCbyN is downloaded from the Reddit API. Several quantitative views are available and range from a simple time series count and progress in complexity to the application of k-nearest neighbors for comment surfacing. At present, much of the commentary is sourced from the wallstreetbets subreddit. Given the bullish sentiment enherent in wsb, other subreddits have been included in an attempt to broaden the coverage of shared ideas. Process details can be found here.
